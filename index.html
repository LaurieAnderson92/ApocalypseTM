The Rules

About

Apocalypse™ is our new hero LARP game. Against the background of an world sundered and reforged, characters will be challenged to restore and extend their influence and fortune. With monsters pouring out of the ruins of Lundon and further horrors lurking in the old world vaults, the collective player base are the heroes that fate has called upon to face this new threat, Discover the secrets of the old world and avert another calamity.

What is LARP?

LARP or Live Action Role-Playing, refers to a form of interactive storytelling where participants physically act out their characters' actions in a fictional setting. Participants typically create characters with distinct personalities, backgrounds, and abilities that fit the setting to then immerse themselves in a shared imaginary world. Players collaborate to co-create narratives, engage in combat, solve puzzles, and interact with other characters.
Blurring the lines between theater, improvisation, and gaming LARPing can range from small, intimate gatherings to large-scale, organized events, catering to a diverse array of genres and themes, including fantasy, science fiction, historical, and horror.
